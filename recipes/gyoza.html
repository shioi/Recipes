Cheese-Feathered Hanetsuki Gyōza

Description

Gyōza are Japanese pan-fried dumplings quite similar to Chinese potstickers.

Ingridinets

  • Chicken Wings
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Water
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Cabbage

Steps:

  1. Prepare the ingridients
  2. Combine the beef, shrimp, cabbage, ginger, garlic, salt, white pepper, and the oils into a large bowl.
  3. Mix everything together well with your hands, making sure the mixture isn't lumpy or uneven
  4. Take a gyoza wrapper and place a small spoonful of the mixture in the middle.
  5. Oil and heat a skillet on the stove.
  6. Fry the dumplings for about two minutes, then reduce the heat.
  7. Add about half a cup of water to the skillet and cover it with a pan lid
  8. Place the dumplings onto a plate or serving bowl and serve with dipping sauce or soy sauce (shoyu)
